# ButterCut Pro Installer

Thanks for buying ButterCut Pro!

**To install:** open this folder in the **Claude** desktop app — the **Cowork**
tab is the easiest place (Claude Code works too) — and say:

> Install ButterCut Pro.

Have two things from your purchase receipt ready:

1. The **email address** you bought with
2. Your **license key**

Claude will ask for both, download ButterCut Pro onto your Mac, and walk you
through the rest. The whole thing takes about five minutes.
